Welcome to Revenue Manager!

This app lets you track and analyze all your income in one place. This quick guide will show you how to get started.

Dashboard
Revenues
Charts
Categories
🏷️

Step 1 — Categories

Categories help you organize your income. Here are some categories you can use:

💼 Salary
💻 Freelance
📈 Investments
🏠 Rental income

You can create your own categories in the Categories section.

➕

Step 2 — Add a revenue

To record a revenue, click the "Add revenue" button at the top right.

1 Enter the amount
2 Choose a category
3 Add a description
4 Select the date
5 Click Save ✅
📊

Step 3 — Analyze your revenues

Once your revenues are added, two sections help you analyze them:

Dashboard Monthly summary, annual total, latest entries
Charts Monthly trend, breakdown by category
🚀

You are ready!

You can also export your data as PDF or CSV from the Export section.

If you need to review this guide, you can relaunch it from Settings → Getting started guide.

Enjoy managing your revenues!
